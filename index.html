The Drosophila random Gene name Generator

My name is Victor, I am a PhD student working with Drosophila melanogaster and my passion is Fun Drosophila Gene Names. Drosophila genes are named by the scientists who first discovered their function. The convention wants that the name should reflect the phenotype of the fly carrying a mutation for this gene. Drosophila scientist can be very imaginative to find new gene names and that is what I want to advertize.

Why this website?

  1. Learn everyday new scientific facts on my favorite model organism
  2. Advertize unknown gene and their potential interest
  3. Not let funny Drosophila gene names unoticed

The Drosophila Genome contains 22,000 genes. If I tweet one genes everyday, it would take 60 years to complete the entire Genome! We better start right away!
